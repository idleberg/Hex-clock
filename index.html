Help

Basics

Use the sliders in the menu to adjust the various filter settings and font-size. You can also specify any local or Google Fonts-hosted font-family and optionally provide its font-weight separated by a colon. Use semantic weights for local fonts (e.g. Menlo:bold) and numerical weights for Google Fonts (e.g. Fira Mono:700, Lato).

Save/Load

All changes are transparently saved to your browser's localStorage and reloaded automatically on future visits.

Reset

Double-click on a label to reset a setting to its initial state. Use tap-hold on touch devices.
